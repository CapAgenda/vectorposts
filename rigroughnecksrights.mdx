Rigs, Roughnecks, Rights" is a captivating vector artwork that explores the world of
oil rigs and the dedicated roughnecks who work tirelessly to extract this valuable resource.
Through bold lines and vibrant colors, this piece showcases the industrial beauty and challenges
faced in the oil industry. It also raises important questions about the rights and safety of workers
in this field, emphasizing the importance of fair treatment and representation.
